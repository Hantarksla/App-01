# User Personas

## Persona 1: Founder

Profile:
- Startup builder
- Works independently

Pain points:
- Lack of external accountability
- Long feedback cycles

Needs:
- Daily progress tracking
- Silent motivation

## Persona 2: Fitness Builder

Profile:
- Wants consistent exercise

Pain points:
- Motivation decreases over time

Needs:
- Progress visualization
- Friend challenge

## Persona 3: Skill Learner

Profile:
- Learning programming, language or certification

Pain points:
- Difficult long-term consistency

Needs:
- Daily small wins
- AI feedback

## Common Behavior

Users prefer:

- Private progress
- Low social pressure
- Fast interaction
- Visible improvement
