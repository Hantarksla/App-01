# Market Research

## Market Position

Momentum AI is positioned between productivity apps, habit trackers and social accountability platforms.

## Existing Categories

### Habit Tracking

Examples:
- Streaks
- Productive
- Habitica

Strength:
- Personal tracking

Weakness:
- Limited social motivation

### Social Fitness

Strength:
- Community motivation

Weakness:
- Often creates social pressure

## Differentiation

Momentum AI focuses on:

- Private accountability
- No chat pressure
- Real actions instead of posts
- Apple Watch interaction

## Opportunity

Growing markets:

- Personal productivity
- Fitness tracking
- AI coaching
- Wearable technology

## Strategy

Start with Apple ecosystem users because:

- Higher subscription willingness
- Strong wearable adoption
- Better payment conversion
